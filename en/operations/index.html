OUR OPERATIONS

Turning Operations into Systems

Not intuition, but systems. Not experience, but data. SOP Standards, Real-Time Control, and Data Reporting — the three pillars of GroundK operations.

Three Pillars of Operations

PILLAR 01

SOP Standards

From pre-event briefings to on-site execution and emergency response, every procedure is documented and standardized. A framework that guarantees consistent quality regardless of who operates.

  • SOP templates tailored to each event type
  • Driver and field manager briefing checklists
  • Emergency response manuals
PILLAR 02

Real-Time Control

Vehicle locations, boarding status, and traffic conditions are monitored in real time from the control center. Immediate response capability for any contingency.

  • GPS-based vehicle tracking
  • Live boarding status aggregation
  • Automated incident alerts and response
PILLAR 03

Reporting

Operational data is collected and analyzed to deliver reports that include quantitative performance metrics and improvement proposals. The foundation for making every next event better.

  • Automated trip data collection
  • KPI achievement dashboard
  • Improvement proposal reports
